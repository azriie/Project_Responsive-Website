About Us

Nike,Inc. is a multinational corporation known for its iconic sportswear and athletic footwear.

Just Do It

The famous Nike Swoosh logo was designed by a graphic designer named Carolyn Davidson in 1971. It has since become one of the most recognizable logos in the world. Nike is primarily known for its athletic footwear, including running shoes, basketball shoes, and sneakers. They also produce a wide range of sports apparel, equipment, and accessories, catering to various sports and activities. Nike faces competition from other major athletic apparel and footwear companies like Adidas, Under Armour, Puma, and Reebok.
